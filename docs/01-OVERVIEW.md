# 01 - What is Pathster?

## The Problem

RVCC students walk around campus confused. They don't know where classes are. They miss the location of the radio station.

Many students get lost or arrive late to class. The campus needs a better way to help them.

## The Solution

Pathster is one app that solves this problem. It combines a campus map with live radio streaming.

Three main features:

1. **Interactive Campus Map** — Find any classroom using GPS
2. **Live Radio Streaming** — Listen to Radio Club broadcasts
3. **Community Data Collection** — Students add missing locations and earn rewards

All three work on phones, tablets, and web browsers.

## How It Works (Simple Version)

A student opens the app at 9:00 AM. She needs to find room 316 in the Science building.

She taps the "Map" button. The app shows her current location on the map.

She searches for "Science 316". The map zooms there and shows her the route.

She can also listen to the campus radio while navigating.

Later, she spots a new classroom not on the map. She submits it with a photo.

Pathster rewards her with points toward free meals.

## Who Benefits?

- **New students** — Understand campus layout quickly
- **Campus visitors** — Navigate without asking for directions
- **Radio Club members** — Stream broadcasts to the whole community
- **Staff** — Track what students care about

## The Technology Stack

| Part | Technology | Why We Chose It |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Frontend** | React Native + Expo | Works on iPhone, Android, and browsers |
| **Backend** | Express.js + TypeScript | Simple and reliable for teams |
| **Database** | MongoDB | Flexible for storing various data types |
| **Caching** | Redis | Fast performance even with many users |
| **Version Control** | Git + GitHub | Industry standard for teams |

All code is TypeScript. This catches bugs before the app runs.
